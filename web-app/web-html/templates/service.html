Our Services

"Together offers a range of services dedicated to disaster management. We provide timely predictions and alerts for potential disasters, ensuring that individuals and communities stay informed and prepared. Our platform also connects users with essential resources and support, facilitating pre-disaster management efforts. Additionally, we host educational videos that enhance awareness and preparedness, empowering everyone to respond effectively in times of crisis. The services we provide are mentioned below. You can click on the icon inorder to use the facility."

Heatwave

Analyze temperature data and forecast heatwaves to help communities prepare and mitigate the risks of extreme heat.

RainFall

Analyze rainfall patterns and provide insights

Earthquake

Analyze seismic data to identify patterns and predict earthquakes, helping communities prepare and respond effectively

Drought

Predict drought conditions accurately to support effective agricultural planning and resource management.

Landslide

Assess geological and environmental factors to predict landslides, enabling timely alerts and proactive measures to safeguard communities
